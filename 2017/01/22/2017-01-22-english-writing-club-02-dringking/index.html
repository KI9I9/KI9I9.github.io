What kind person are you when you drink

发表于 2017-01-22   |   分类于 English

At this week’s Friday night, our company held an annual festival in a hotel.Since our company is a state-owned enterprise, you can’t avoid drinking with your colleagues and boss.

This was the second year-end dinner party I have spent with these people.I can always find something interesting at this time of the year.I mean when around the table with wine on it, people always show something you will not or cannot realize in daily communications.

You know, usually the more you can drink, the more you are welcomed at the dinner (at least most leaders in my company do).Among the sound of ‘cheers’ and the clink of cups, those who drink a lot are always the focus of people’s eyes.However, he is definitely not the only one you will notice.With everyone’s emotions up there, people not only open their appetite but also open their mind to each other.Then you hear what you never heard, you see what you never saw.

The driver sat next to the table where our boss was.He stood up and held his wine glass, spoke loudly to the general manager:”Today is a happy day, we all think you should give some more red envelopes by Wechat.Everyone, let’s drink this one to Manager L.Bottooms up! “ And after several other good words, our manager was happy and, yes, gave some more red envelopes in our chat group, over and over.I never thought the driver would do this.No offense, but as is known to me, he is just a driver who usually drive to pick up the guests to our company or take them to the hotel.Speaking straightforwardly to the boss like this way is very brave, even a little rude.Then I realized maybe he was the one who knew our manager’s mind most clearly at that night.Manager L need and enjoyed the feels of being flattered.He was also happy to spend a little money to make his employees’ praise his generosity.He wanted people here to remember that he is a kind and generous boss.You see, those who help you most in your business may not necessarily be the one knows your selfness, but there has to be one who does.

Some of mine other colleagues, whom I’m familiar with or not, also showed another side of their characters that night.Young or old, men or women, employers or employees.Someone’s character was scaled up.Someone hide something in his mind but been disclosed unconsciously by others’ word.Of course, someone knew how to protect his own safety no matter what happened or what other said.

I don’t mean to hurt or criticize anyone of them.Because I can see every one of them in my own body.What I need to do is change what I can and accept what I cannot.The difference between them is what I will learn in my whole life.
