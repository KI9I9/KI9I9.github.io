Responsbility

发表于 2017-01-29   |   分类于 English

I always believe that if a person is good enough and has a strong heart to work hard, then time will prove that he will achieve the success he wants.However, recently I start to think about this in another angle.

Does it still have any meaning to your life if the success you want comes too late? I ask myself about this question not because I doubt the importance of hard-working.On the contrary, I know if one desire to succeed in anything, he must pay the price.But things change so quickly that life always gives you inadequate time to get what you want.Not because what you desire is too big or too difficult to achieve, but that some successes themselves have timeliness.

For instance, I’m very close to my grandma and she is in no condition.I always worry about her health.I have been working in another city since I graduated from the college. I just come back home several times all a year, which made me always think I’m not a filial grandson.To be honest, my job is not very good, even myself think my choice is so conservative.In my whole family, I’m the only man who hasn’t got married at the right age.Considering only a few of my friends and classmates around me has married and the dream I want to realize make me think marriage is not the most important and urgent thing to do.Nonetheless, when I notice my grandma’s health is going worse and worse, I became to blame myself.I can wait, my parents can wait but how could I let my grandma wait? Her last wish is to witness me find a good wife, lives a happy life and gives birth to a healthy child.She never makes me hard or forces me to have a blind date, which makes me more self-condemned.So what could I do?

I give myself an answer: keep on doing what you want and then just move forward a step further.Find another job which you are willing to fight for, not necessarily with a good salary right now, but must have a good future.Going out and giving myself an opportunity to make new friends.Sitting on the couch or indulging myself on the internet won’t give me a girlfriend.True love is never easy to find, especially when you consider it will find you naturally.

Sometimes, a little more push is necessary for your own good.
