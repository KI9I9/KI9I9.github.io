Spring Festival

发表于 2017-01-28   |   分类于 English

Several days ago, I went away from Nanjing and back to my hometown.With my family, I spent a happy Lunar New Year’s Eve yesterday.

If you have seen this year’s Spring Festival Gala Evening, you know it is not good enough.People, especially young fellows, their taste is never easy to satisfy today.But this is also the reason why many people watch it these years just like I do.We made complaints and share the opinions why we don’t like a particular performance with our friends or net friends in Weibo or Wechat.The sound of laughing and bangs of firecrackers and fireworks continued until midnight. We can always figure out a way to relax, be happy and entertain ourselves.Happiness is never hidden when you get friends and family around.

With Wechat’s announcement to quit, this year’s War of Red Envelope seems not as fierce as last year’s.However, people’s complaint and outcry to Alipay didn’t decrease.Countless people collect all five “Fu” difficultly and expect to share the total 200 million bonus.People always believe they will be the lucky ones who finally win the prize.Even people with high education seem like have forgotten how lower the chances were.As far as I am concerned, it was a successful marketing strategy for Alipay, but a true failure because the image of corporate was affected every year.Lost the user’s heart, you lost everything.Your mistake will be zoomed in when your competitor is on the right way.

What another thing I appreciate the Spring Festival is, it gives me a chance to have a reunion with my friends, who also earn their living by working hard in other big cities.We may not connect with each other frequently, but the friendship between us has never faded away due to the long distance.

Wish all of you have a good festival with your family and friends.
